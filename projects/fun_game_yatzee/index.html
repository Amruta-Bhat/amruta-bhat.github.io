How to Play

  • Roll the dice up to 3 times per turn.
  • Click dice to hold them between rolls.
  • Select a category to assign your dice after rolling.
  • Each category can be used only once.
  • Score the best combinations to win!
  • Get 63+ points in upper section for a 35 point bonus.
  • Yahtzee = five of a kind (worth 50 points).
